Our Signal, Our Guide

Our mission is to help students in Rexburg, Idaho save money by finding and sharing the best local deals and sales. As a business run by students for students, we are committed to supporting our community, making everyday life more affordable, and helping others while working together to create value for everyone.

Background

We are a collective of students at Brigham Young University. When prompted to build a website and an app, we decided to look towards our community, identify a need, and try our best to offer a solution. It is with this in mind that we offer you Rick's Radar!

What is Ricks Radar?

Ricks Radar is way for students at BYUI to find localized sales and discounts. It works by users like you submitting deals as you find them walking about. Deals will also be added by our team as we discover them. It functions like a social media app in that you can scroll to view already submitted deals, and can post ones you find.
